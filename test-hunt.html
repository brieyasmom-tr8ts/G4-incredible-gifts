Scavenger Hunt!

1. Browse the prompts and pick the ones that look fun — no need to do them all!
2. Tap a prompt, snap a photo (or choose from camera roll), and add a caption. Group photo? Tag everyone's names in the caption!
3. Switch to Gallery & Vote to see what everyone captured. Vote for your favorite in each category!
4. You get one vote per category — choose wisely! You can change your vote until 7 PM.
5. Be creative! Winners announced tonight. Your photos will be in the slideshow! 🎉
